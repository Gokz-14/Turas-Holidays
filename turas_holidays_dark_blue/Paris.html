Romantic Paris Getaway

Embark on a 7-day romantic adventure through the heart of Paris. Explore the city’s iconic landmarks, indulge in world-class French cuisine, and relax on a luxurious Seine River cruise. Whether it's admiring the Eiffel Tower or wandering the cobbled streets of Montmartre, this getaway promises unforgettable moments.

Package Highlights

Visit the Iconic Eiffel Tower

Enjoy breathtaking views of Paris from the Eiffel Tower’s observation deck. A must-see during your romantic getaway.

Seine River Cruise

Relax with a serene boat ride down the Seine, passing under the famous bridges while enjoying dinner and fine wine.

Stroll through Montmartre

Wander the charming streets of Montmartre, the artistic district of Paris, and enjoy local cafes and art galleries.
